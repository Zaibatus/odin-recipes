Creamy chicken piccata

Ingredients

For the chicken breast

  • Chicken breast16 oz (450 g)
  • Flour1 cup (115 g)
  • Salt½ tsp (5 g)
  • Black pepper¼ tbsp (2 g)
  • Extra virgin olive oil2 ½ tbsp (40 ml)
  • Butter1 tbsp (15 g)

For the sauce

  • Butter3 tbsp (45 g)
  • Extra virgin olive oil2 tbsp (30 ml)
  • White wine⅔ cup (100 ml)
  • Lemon juice2 tbsp (20 ml)
  • Salted capers3 tbsp (40 g) - (drained)
  • Parsley1 tsp (5 g) - (fresh, chopped)

Preparation

  1. Cut chicken breast into 3 pieces, then pound until 0.2 inch thick. If you use chicken fillets it is not necessary to cut just pound each piece.
  2. Put flour in a dish together with salt and pepper and mix. Dip each piece of chicken, press into flour to stick, shake off excess.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon butter and 2 1/2 tablespoons oil in a large skillet until butter is melted.
  4. Sauté chicken and cook until golden brown (about 2-3 minutes per side). Once cooked remove from pan.
  5. In a clean frying pan, place 2 tablespoons of butter and melt but do not become foamy. Add wine and simmer until reduced by half (about 1 minute).
  6. Put lemon juice in the pan and simmer 1 minute, the sauce should become translucent and slightly thickened. To finish the sauce, add capers and parsley.
  7. Put the chicken breasts back in the pan with the sauce for a few seconds to add flavour.
  8. Turn off the stove and serve chicken piccata garnished with fresh chopped parsley.
