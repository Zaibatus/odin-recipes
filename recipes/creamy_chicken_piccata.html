Creamy chicken piccata

Ingredients

For the chicken breast

  • Chicken breast16 oz (450 g)
  • Flour1 cup (115 g)
  • Salt½ tsp (5 g)
  • Black pepper¼ tbsp (2 g)
  • Extra virgin olive oil2 ½ tbsp (40 ml)
  • Butter1 tbsp (15 g)

For the sauce

  • Butter3 tbsp (45 g)
  • Extra virgin olive oil2 tbsp (30 ml)
  • White wine⅔ cup (100 ml)
  • Lemon juice2 tbsp (20 ml)
  • Salted capers3 tbsp (40 g) - (drained)
  • Parsley1 tsp (5 g) - (fresh, chopped)
